Jesus Loves You

The love of God is boundless, unconditional, and eternal, One of the most profound expressions of God's love is found in John 3:16 God's love is not based on our actions or worthiness. As we experience God's love, we are called to share it with others.
Take a moment today to reflect on the depth of God's love for you

"For God so loved the world that he gave his only begotten Son..."

Prayer of Salvation

8 But what saith it? The word is nigh thee, even in thy mouth, and in thy heart: that is, the word of faith, which we preach;
9 That if thou shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus, and shalt believe in thine heart that God hath raised him from the dead, thou shalt be saved.
10 For with the heart man believeth unto righteousness; and with the mouth confession is made unto salvation.
11 For the scripture saith, Whosoever believeth on him shall not be ashamed.

Romans 10 8-11

If you desire to receive Jesus Christ as your Lord and Savior, please pray this prayer sincerely:

"Dear Lord Jesus, I acknowledge that I am a sinner in need of Your grace. I believe that You died for my sins and rose again on the third day. I confess You as my Lord and Savior, and I invite You into my heart. Please forgive me, cleanse me, and help me to live for You from this day forward. Amen."

If you prayed this prayer, we rejoice with you! You have taken the first step in your journey of faith. We encourage you to connect with us so we can support you in your new walk with Christ.

We can't wait to see you! If you have any questions or need more information, please contact us with any of the options provided below. May God bless you abundantly as you seek Him.

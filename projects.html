Projects, Partnerships & Giving

Our Projects

  • Community Food Outreach: Providing meals and essential supplies to families in need within our local community.
  • Youth Empowerment Program: Mentoring, skills training, and educational support for young people to help them discover and fulfill their potential.
  • Medical Missions: Organizing free health clinics and medical outreach to underserved areas, offering care and hope.
  • Church Building & Renovation: Expanding and maintaining our facilities to create a welcoming space for worship, learning, and fellowship.
  • Evangelism & Missions: Supporting missionaries and outreach teams to share the Gospel locally and internationally.

How to Partner With Us

We invite you to join hands with us in making a lasting impact. Here’s how you can partner with Revived Christian Life Ministry:

  • Pray: Stand with us in prayer for our projects, our leaders, and those we serve.
  • Volunteer: Use your gifts and time to serve in our outreach programs, events, or ministry teams.
  • Give: Support our work financially through one-time or recurring donations.
  • Share: Spread the word about our mission and invite others to get involved.
  • Partner as an Organization: If you represent a business or group, contact us to explore partnership opportunities.

To learn more or get involved, please contact us or speak with a ministry leader.

Why We Give

Giving isn’t just a donation — it’s worship. Generosity is a natural response to God's overwhelming love and grace. When we give, we’re not just supporting a church — we’re investing in spreading the Gospel, changing lives, and advancing the Kingdom of God both near and far. Your giving is an act of faith and obedience, reflecting trust in God's provision and a desire to make a difference. Every gift, no matter the size, helps us reach more people, serve our community, and fulfill our mission.

“Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver.”

— 2 Corinthians 9:7

Your giving fuels:

Outreach to our community — feeding the hungry, clothing the poor, and offering hope to the hurting.

Missions beyond our walls — taking the Good News to the unreached and forgotten.

Ministry growth — equipping children, youth, and adults to walk in their God-given purpose.

Facilities and resources — providing a welcoming space where lives are transformed.
